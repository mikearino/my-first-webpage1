Mexico is rad and the Cenotes are so unbelievably cool! They are underground caves full of crystal clear water.

Hawaii

Hawaii is an amazing place. If I could be anywhere right now, I would be snorkeling on one of their beaches, getting blasted by the sun.

Japan

Japan is so cool! They embrace technology and have so many wonderful bits to offer. I visited there in highschool and I would really love to go back someday.
